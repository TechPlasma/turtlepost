Catalog Book Request Monthly Challenge

I've always rather enjoyed reading and I had come across a bunch of these little libraries spread out in various places. I think the first ones I saw were up in Chicago when I was visiting famliy there. And when I moved to Oklahoma I happened across a great many of them here as well. I was enamored by the concept and I made it a personal goal to build one for my place.

It was a fantastic project and I had a lot of fun doing it. And honestly it came out a lot better than I could have possibly imagined.
